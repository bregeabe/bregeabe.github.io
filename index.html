Projects

Programmable Quantum Circuit Simulator

React.js, Python, Qiskit

A simulator geared towards lowering the barrier to entry for aspiring students who wish to get involved in quantum information science. PQCV currently supports the Hadamard, Phase shift, and Pauli operations on a single qubit. The kick is the animation of the qubit changing state across the screen in real time, along with multiple mathematical representations at the bottom, accompanied by an interactive bloch sphere showing the current state vector. Originally created for myself, my goal now is to continue development to support mutli-qubit operations and gates.

Serpent

Next.js, TypeScript

Serpent is like Strava for programmers. It tracks your coding activity on websites like GitHub and Leetcode and allows you to create your own projects for a personalized feel. On top of that, it introduces the aspect of time, a concept not typically associated with coding. By visualizing your coding activity over time, Serpent helps you identify patterns and trends in your development.
